<h3>Academic Background</h3>
<p>I completed my undergraduate degree in Linguistics at <a href="https://www.reed.edu/about-reed/">Reed College</a> in 2019. Through that experience, I developed a love of working with unstructured data and being able to tell compelling stories through the power of computing. More information about my linguistics work can be found <a href="/linguistics">here</a>.</p>

<p>In 2021, I joined the <a href="https://cs.illinois.edu/academics/graduate/ican">iCAN</a> program at the <a href="https://cs.illinois.edu/">University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign</a> (UIUC), a bridge program designed to broaden access to computing. After iCAN, I was accepted to the Master's program at UIUC to continue my computer science education and graduated with an MS in Computer Science in May of 2023. More information about projects I completed during my time at UIUC can be found <a href="/UIUCProjects">here</a>.</p>

<h3>DREAM Fellowship</h3>
<p>While in the iCAN program at UIUC, I was fortunate to be a <a href="https://tech.mines.edu/dream/">DREAM</a> Fellow and conduct research on the nature of scientific impact with <a href="https://cs.illinois.edu/about/people/faculty/chackoge">Dr. George Chacko</a>. I was able to devote 400 hours of work on an independent project of my choice through the support of the DREAM program and I chose to report on different metrics for evaluating the impact of scientific studies. For more information, please see my <a href="https://el-wittmer.github.io/dream">project website.</a></p>
